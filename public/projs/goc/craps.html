Nevada Craps

Standard Table Rules:

A 7 or 11 on the first roll wins

A 2, 3, 12 on the first roll loses

Any other number rolled becomes the "point."

You continue to roll; if you get your point you win.

If you roll a 7, you lose and the dice changes hands.

Note: You're only playing to win or lose.
